What is this thing?

imgmkr can be used to generate all of your social media images from one single image. You can change images out individually, or upload one master image to change all at once. Images can be scaled and adjusted within the frame by dragging them.

Once your images have been adjusted, download only the items you need, or download all at once in a zip file.
